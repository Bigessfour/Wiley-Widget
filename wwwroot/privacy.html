Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 13, 2025

Overview

Wiley Widget ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our desktop application.

Information We Collect

Wiley Widget integrates with QuickBooks Online and xAI's Grok API to provide municipal financial management and AI-powered insights. We collect:

  • QuickBooks Data: Financial data, customer information, and accounting records through authorized API access
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth tokens to maintain secure connection with your QuickBooks account
  • Application Settings: Your preferences and configuration settings stored locally
  • AI Usage Data: Queries sent to xAI Grok API for analysis and insights (anonymized and not stored)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide municipal financial management and reporting services
  • Synchronize data between Wiley Widget and QuickBooks Online
  • Generate financial reports and analytics
  • Maintain secure access to your QuickBooks company data
  • Process anonymized data through xAI Grok API for AI-powered insights and recommendations
  • Enhance municipal decision-making with AI-generated analysis

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your computer or in your authorized QuickBooks account
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely using Windows Credential Manager
  • We do not transmit your financial data to external servers (except QuickBooks API)
  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption

AI Data Processing

When you use AI-powered features:

  • Data Anonymization: Personal identifiers are removed before sending data to xAI
  • Processing Location: AI processing occurs on xAI's secure servers
  • Data Retention: AI queries are not stored; only anonymized insights are returned
  • Purpose Limitation: AI processing is limited to providing municipal financial insights
  • GDPR Compliance: We ensure AI data processing complies with GDPR requirements for automated decision-making

Third-Party Services

Wiley Widget integrates with:

  • QuickBooks Online: Intuit's accounting platform (see Intuit Privacy Policy)
  • xAI Grok API: xAI's AI service for data analysis and insights (see xAI Privacy Policy)
  • Syncfusion: UI component library

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke QuickBooks access at any time through the application Settings
  • Delete all local application data
  • Access and export your data from QuickBooks directly

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide services. You can delete application data at any time by:

  • Disconnecting from QuickBooks in application Settings
  • Uninstalling the application
  • Manually deleting application data folder

Children's Privacy

Wiley Widget is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Effective Date."
